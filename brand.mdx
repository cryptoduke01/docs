## Voice

- Speak in **plain, sharp language**.
- Focus on **events**, **markets**, and **memes** rather than low-level protocol details.
- Emphasize that Siren connects **prediction markets** and **tokens**.

Examples:

- "Launch an event-driven meme token for the election."
- "See which memes are actually priced by the market."

---

## Colors

Siren uses a dark theme with purple as the primary accent.

- Primary: `#a855f7`
- Primary light: `#c4b5fd`
- Primary dark: `#4c1d95`
- Background: near-black (`#050508` to `#0a0a0c`)

Use a dark background with purple accents for most brand surfaces.

## How to pitch Siren

Short pitch:

> Siren is a terminal that lets you trade Kalshi markets, surface and launch Bags meme tokens tied to those events, and share your performance with a clean PnL card.

Longer pitch:

- Markets: Browse Kalshi markets with live probabilities and velocity.
- Tokens: See which Bags tokens map to those events, or launch your own.
- Trades: Trade markets via DFlow and tokens via Jupiter.
- Portfolio: Track SOL, tokens, prediction positions, and Bags fee earnings.
- Social: Share a branded PnL card for any token + market pair.
