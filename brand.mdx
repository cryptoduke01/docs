## Voice

- Speak in **plain, sharp language**.
- Focus on **events**, **markets**, and **memes** rather than low-level protocol details.
- Emphasize that Siren connects **prediction markets** and **tokens** with a strong execution and distribution layer.

Examples:

- "Launch an event-driven meme token for the election."
- "See which memes are actually priced by the market."

## Colors

Siren uses a near-black base with electric green as the primary action color and Polymarket purple as a secondary rail color.

![Siren brand styles](/images/brand/styles.png)

- Action green: `#00FF85`
- Kalshi rail: `#5DDFB0`
- Polymarket rail: `#6B3FDB`
- Surface: `#0F0F18`
- Elevated surface: `#141420`
- Void background: `#060609`

Use the green for actions and live-state emphasis. Use purple to distinguish the Polymarket rail, not as the overall brand base.

## How to pitch Siren

Short pitch:

> Siren is a terminal that watches Kalshi and Polymarket, maps those market moves into Solana meme tokens, and lets you act on the narrative fast.

Longer pitch:

- Markets: Watch Kalshi and Polymarket in one feed.
- Tokens: See which Solana meme assets map to those events, or launch your own.
- Trades: Execute the Solana token flow directly in Siren.
- Portfolio: Track Solana and Base wallet rails, prediction exposure, and Bags fee earnings.
- Social: Share a branded market card with `onsiren.xyz` baked into the asset.
