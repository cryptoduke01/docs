# Siren

Siren is an **event-driven trading terminal** for the Solana meme economy.

It connects:

- **Prediction markets** from **Kalshi** and **Polymarket**
- **Matched Solana tokens** from **DexScreener** and **Bags**
- **Market and token execution** from one interface
- **Portfolio tools** for Solana plus built-in EVM wallets, including Base deposits

> App: [onsiren.xyz](https://onsiren.xyz)  
> Docs: [docs.onsiren.xyz](https://docs.onsiren.xyz)

---

## What you can do

- **Track live market moves**  
  Siren watches Kalshi and Polymarket at the same time, ranks sharp market moves, and shows the source on every signal card.

- **Surface tokens by event**  
  Siren maps plain-English market questions to Solana meme tokens and launch candidates.

- **Trade the token flow fast**  
  Buy and sell linked Solana tokens, launch new Bags tokens, and move from prediction signal to token execution without leaving the terminal.

- **Track your portfolio**  
  Track Solana balances, your Base deposit address, prediction positions, token holdings, and Bags fee earnings in one place.

- **Share the market narrative**  
  Export the selected market card as a branded Siren image with `onsiren.xyz` baked into the asset for social distribution.

## How Siren works

### Live signal feed

- Kalshi and Polymarket run in parallel.
- Signals are ranked by live movement size.
- Every signal carries source, delta, timestamp, and matched token context.

### Wallets

- Privy handles sign-in with **Google**, **GitHub**, and **X**.
- Siren provisions an embedded **Solana** wallet for terminal execution.
- Siren also provisions an embedded **EVM** wallet for **Base / Ethereum / Polygon** support.

### Token view

- DexScreener and Bags power token discovery.
- Launch directly into the narrative when a market has no clear meme asset.
- Keep the execution path focused on Solana liquidity while markets provide the signal.
