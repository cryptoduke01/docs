# Siren

Siren is an **event-driven meme token terminal** on Solana.

It connects:

- **Prediction markets** (via Kalshi/DFlow)
- **Meme tokens** (via Bags and DexScreener)
- **Swaps & execution** (via Jupiter)
- **Your portfolio & PnL** (including a shareable PnL card)

> App: [onsiren.xyz](https://onsiren.xyz)  
> Docs: [docs.onsiren.xyz](https://docs.onsiren.xyz)

---

## What you can do

- **Browse prediction markets**  
  Live probability, volume, and velocity from Kalshi via DFlow.

- **Surface tokens by event**  
  Siren searches for **Bags tokens** and other Solana tokens whose narratives match each market.

- **Trade markets and tokens**  
  - Buy YES/NO on markets (via DFlow)
  - Swap tokens (via Jupiter)
  - Launch new Bags tokens directly from Siren

- **Track your portfolio**  
  SOL, tokens, prediction positions, and Bags fee earnings in one place.

- **Share your PnL**  
  Generate a **branded PnL card** for a token + its linked Kalshi market and share or download it.

---

## The Siren x Bags token

Siren integrates closely with **Bags**, a protocol for launching and routing liquidity to meme tokens.

- Launch new tokens as **Bags tokens** directly from the Siren Launch flow.
- Earn and track **Bags fee share** in your Siren portfolio.
- Surface your token next to relevant prediction markets for organic discovery.
