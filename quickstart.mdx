# Quickstart

This guide gets you from **0 → Siren terminal** in a few minutes.

---

## 1. Get an access code

Siren is currently gated.

1. Join the waitlist at [onsiren.xyz/waitlist](https://onsiren.xyz/waitlist).
2. When you’re approved, you’ll receive a **6‑digit access code** by email.
3. Go to [onsiren.xyz/access](https://onsiren.xyz/access) and enter your code.

If you lose your code, reply to the welcome email or contact the Siren team.

---

## 2. Connect or sign in

On [onsiren.xyz](https://onsiren.xyz):

### Connect a Solana wallet

- **Supported:** Phantom, Solflare, Torus (via Solana Wallet Adapter).
- Once connected, Siren tracks:
  - Your SOL balance
  - Tokens
  - Prediction positions
  - Bags fee earnings

### Sign in with Google / GitHub / X

If you don’t want to install a wallet yet, you can sign in with:

- **Google**
- **GitHub**
- **X (Twitter)** (once configured)

Behind the scenes, Siren uses **Dynamic** to create an **embedded Solana wallet** for you. You can later export keys or connect a full wallet.

---

## 3. Explore the Terminal

Once you’re in:

1. Open the **Terminal** tab.
2. Use filters to browse markets by category:
   - Politics
   - Crypto
   - Macro
   - Sports
   - Other events
3. Click a market to open its detail view:
   - Live probability
   - Volume and velocity
   - Linked Bags tokens and other Solana tokens

---

## 4. Trade a market or token

### Trade a prediction market

- Use the unified buy/sell panel to buy **YES** or **NO** on a Kalshi market via DFlow.
- Siren handles:
  - Quotes
  - Slippage
  - Transaction construction

> Note: Siren enforces geo‑fencing for **US and restricted Kalshi jurisdictions** where required.

### Swap a token

- From a market’s linked tokens or from the **Trending / Portfolio** views:
  - Choose a token
  - Use the swap panel (powered by Jupiter) to buy or sell.

---

## 5. Share your PnL

In the **Portfolio → PnL card**:

- Pick a token and its linked Kalshi market.
- Siren generates a **shareable, branded PnL card** with:
  - Token symbol
  - PnL and percentage
  - Market name
  - Siren branding + `onsiren.xyz`

You can **download** the card as an image or share it directly.

---

## 6. Launch a new Bags token

When you see an event with no obvious token:

1. Go to the **Launch** tab.
2. Pre‑fill from a market (optional) to inherit the narrative.
3. Configure:
   - Name, symbol, description
   - Social links (X, Telegram, website)
   - Image and branding
4. Siren wires this to **Bags** for launch, liquidity, and fee share.

See the [Launch](./launch) page for a full walkthrough.
