# Quickstart

This guide gets you from **0 → Siren terminal** in a few minutes.

---

## 1. Get an access code

Siren is currently gated.

1. Join the waitlist at [onsiren.xyz/waitlist](https://onsiren.xyz/waitlist).
2. When you’re approved, you’ll receive a **6‑digit access code** by email.
3. Go to [onsiren.xyz/access](https://onsiren.xyz/access) and enter your code.

If you lose your code, reply to the welcome email or contact the Siren team.

---

## 2. Sign in with Privy

On [onsiren.xyz](https://onsiren.xyz):

- **Google**
- **GitHub**
- **X**

Behind the scenes, Siren uses **Privy** to provision:

- An embedded **Solana** wallet for terminal execution
- An embedded **EVM** wallet rail for **Base / Ethereum / Polygon**

If you later want self-custody, you can export the embedded execution key from inside the product.

---

## 3. Explore the Terminal

Once you’re in:

1. Open the **Terminal** tab.
2. Watch the live signal feed:
   - **ALL**
   - **KALSHI**
   - **POLYMARKET**
3. Use filters to browse markets by category:
   - Politics
   - Crypto
   - Sports
   - Business
   - Entertainment
4. Click a market to open its detail card:
   - Live probability
   - Volume and velocity
   - Source rail
   - Linked Solana tokens
   - Share / download export

---

## 4. Trade a token from the market narrative

### Trade the linked token flow

- Select a market.
- Review the linked token surface.
- Use the trade panel to buy or sell the token you want.

Siren handles quotes, routing, and transaction construction for the Solana execution leg.

### Prediction market note

- Kalshi market execution is routed through the prediction market rail and is subject to venue compliance.
- Polymarket is live as a signal source and market context rail.

---

## 5. Open your portfolio

In **Portfolio** you can:

- See your Solana balance
- Copy your **Base** deposit address
- Track prediction positions
- Monitor Bags fee earnings
- Export the embedded wallet private key when needed

---

## 6. Launch a new Bags token

When you see an event with no obvious token:

1. Go to the **Launch** tab.
2. Pre‑fill from a market (optional) to inherit the narrative.
3. Configure:
   - Name, symbol, description
   - Social links (X, Telegram, website)
   - Image and branding
4. Siren wires this to **Bags** for launch, liquidity, and fee share.

See the [Launch](./essentials/launch) page for a full walkthrough.
