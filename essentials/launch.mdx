# Launch

The **Launch** flow lets you create new **Bags tokens** directly from Siren.

Think of it as a launchpad that is natively aware of prediction markets.

---

## When to launch

Good candidates for a new token:

- A high‑velocity market with no obvious narrative token yet
- An event that crypto cares about, but existing tokens don’t capture
- Experiments around macro, politics, or culture that feel meme‑able

From the Terminal, Siren can suggest launching when a market has
velocity but no surfaced tokens.

---

## Launch steps

1. **Choose a market (optional)**
   - Pre‑fill the launch form from a Kalshi or Polymarket market so the narrative matches.

2. **Set token metadata**
   - Name (e.g. `Siren`)
   - Symbol (e.g. `SIREN`)
   - Description (what the meme is about)
   - Image / logo URL

3. **Add social links**
   - X (Twitter) handle
   - Telegram
   - Website / Mirror post

4. **Confirm and sign**
   - Siren uses Bags to create the token, set up liquidity, and configure fees.
   - You sign the Solana transaction(s) with your wallet or embedded wallet.

5. **Track from Portfolio**
   - Once launched, your new token appears in the Portfolio view and in linked markets.

---

## Fees and revenue

Bags supports configurable fee share. Siren can:

- Route volume to your Bags token
- Surface your token next to relevant markets
- Show fee earnings in the Portfolio view

This makes it easy to see which launches are actually working.

---

## Best practices

- Make the meme clear: name, image, and market should tell one story.
- Link to at least one live prediction market in the description so people see the event context.
- Keep social links active; the Launch flow is just the start.
