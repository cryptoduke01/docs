# Integrations

Siren is a thin, opinionated layer that sits on top of several battle‑tested protocols and services.

This page is the high‑level map.

---

## DFlow (Kalshi markets)

[DFlow](https://pond.dflow.net) is how Siren connects to **Kalshi prediction markets**.

Used for:

- Fetching live markets and probabilities
- Getting quotes for YES/NO trades
- Submitting orders & transactions

Keys & env:

- `DFLOW_API_KEY`
- `DFLOW_METADATA_API_URL`
- `DFLOW_QUOTE_API_URL`

Docs: [DFlow docs](https://dflow.mintlify.app)

---

## Bags (token launch + routing)

[Bags](https://bags.fm) powers **meme token launches** and fee routing.

Used for:

- Creating and configuring new tokens
- Setting partner/fee share
- Surfacing on‑chain token stats

Keys & env:

- `BAGS_API_KEY`
- `BAGS_PARTNER_CONFIG_KEY`
- `BAGS_REF_URL`

Siren Bags token:

- [bags.fm/CQV1aTUmYSGA4NJLzfgyaYHfeMeLYkqh8GtXcW9MBAGS](https://bags.fm/CQV1aTUmYSGA4NJLzfgyaYHfeMeLYkqh8GtXcW9MBAGS)

Docs: [Bags docs](https://docs.bags.fm)

---

## Jupiter (swaps)

[Jupiter](https://jup.ag) is used for **token swaps** inside Siren.

Used for:

- Swapping SOL ↔ tokens
- Routing through the best venues

Env:

- `JUPITER_API_KEY`

Docs: [Jupiter docs](https://station.jup.ag/docs)

---

## Supabase (database + admin)

[Supabase](https://supabase.com) backs Siren’s basic data layer.

Used for:

- **Waitlist** (`waitlist_signups`)
- **Users** (`users` table with wallet, auth user id, signup source, country)
- Admin queries for investor‑ready dashboards

Env:

- `SUPABASE_URL`
- `SUPABASE_SERVICE_ROLE_KEY`

Docs: [Supabase docs](https://supabase.com/docs)

---

## Dynamic (auth + embedded wallets)

[Dynamic](https://www.dynamic.xyz) is used for **OAuth + embedded Solana wallets**.

Used for:

- Social login via Google, GitHub, X
- Creating an embedded Solana wallet per user
- Managing sessions and MFA

Env:

- `NEXT_PUBLIC_DYNAMIC_ENVIRONMENT_ID`

Docs: [Dynamic Solana wallets](https://www.dynamic.xyz/docs/react/wallets/using-wallets/solana/solana-wallets)

---

## Resend (emails)

[Resend](https://resend.com) sends **welcome + access‑code emails** to early users.

Used for:

- Access code emails when admins generate codes for waitlist signups

Env:

- `RESEND_API_KEY`
- `SIREN_EMAIL_FROM`
- `SIREN_APP_URL`

Docs: [Resend Node docs](https://resend.com/docs/send-with-nodejs)

---

## Putting it together

At a high level:

- **Supabase** stores waitlists and users.
- **DFlow** streams markets.
- **Bags** creates tokens and routes fees.
- **Jupiter** trades tokens.
- **Dynamic** authenticates users and provides wallets.
- **Resend** handles email.

Siren’s job is to stitch these together into a **single event‑driven trading experience**.
