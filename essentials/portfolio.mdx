# Portfolio

The **Portfolio** view is your home base for balances, positions, and performance.

---

## Overview

The portfolio aggregates:

- **Solana balance**
- **Base receive balance and address rail**
- **Tokens** held on Solana
- **Prediction positions** from Kalshi (via DFlow)
- **Bags fee earnings** tied to your launches and trades
- **Embedded wallet controls** such as private-key export

You can think of it as a unified view of:

- On‑chain assets
- Prediction exposure
- Protocol revenue from Bags
- Cross-chain account access.

---

## Wallet rails

Siren is Privy-first.

- The embedded **Solana** wallet is used for terminal execution.
- The embedded **EVM** wallet rail gives you a **Base** deposit address.
- Portfolio shows the receive addresses you actually need, without making you leave the product.

For teams and power users, the embedded execution key can be exported from the portfolio / wallet controls.

---

## Tokens & positions

The tokens section shows:

- Token symbol and name
- Balance and USD value (using on‑chain price feeds / Jupiter)
- 24h / 7d performance (when available)

Prediction positions show:

- Market name
- YES/NO side
- Entry price and size
- Mark‑to‑market value based on current probabilities

> Viewing positions does not require KYC. Placing **Kalshi** market trades does. Siren surfaces the exposure either way, but venue compliance still applies before those market orders can execute.

---

## Bags fee earnings

If you launch or route trades through **Bags**, Siren surfaces:

- Total fees earned
- Fees per token you launched
- Links out to the underlying Bags pools and analytics

This makes it easy to see how much your "event‑driven" token experiments have returned.

---

## Receive and send

Portfolio includes fast wallet operations:

- Copy the **Solana** receive address
- Copy the **Base** receive address
- Show QR codes for deposits
- Send Solana assets from the embedded execution rail

This turns Portfolio into an actual account surface, not just a read-only tracker.

---

## Networks & wallets

Portfolio respects the wallet / embedded wallet the user is currently connected with.

- For **browser wallets** (Phantom, Solflare, Torus), balances come from the connected public key.
- For **embedded wallets** (via Privy social login), Siren uses the embedded Solana and EVM wallet rails behind the scenes.

As you connect different wallets, the portfolio view updates accordingly.
